# PressureFlip Solutions Research Notes

## Verified Supplier Context

### NIRA Instruments

- NIRA lists LEL Monitors and Analyzers as product categories.
- NIRA products include Sagittarius 2000, described as flame ionization LEL monitoring systems.
- NIRA products include Sagittarius 5000, described as infrared LEL monitoring systems.
- NIRA lists automatic air recirculation control systems for energy saving and flow-rate reduction in air treatment systems.
- NIRA applications describe LEL monitoring as an analytical system for checking flammable solvent concentrations and keeping them below explosion limits.
- NIRA describes fast residual solvent analyzers as specialized gas chromatograph systems for flexible, coated and laminated printed materials.

### Oggioni DRS

- Oggioni DRS lists fixed gas detectors, gas detection panels, heat detectors, flame detectors, portable gas detectors and visual acoustic alarms.
- Oggioni DRS gas applications include restaurants and commercial kitchens, boiler rooms, warehouses, battery rooms, chemical industries and LPG/oil and gas storage.
- Oggioni DRS RAS-AD eNose4.0 is described as an ATEX Zone 1 gas detector with 4-20 mA, relay and RS485 output options.
- Oggioni DRS TMP2 JO and TO is described as an ATEX Zone 1 / 21 and EN54-5 heat detector.
